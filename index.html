GIS Consulting Services

Our team understands the importance of analyzing and mapping natural features to create a more comprehensive idea of how our interactions are affecting the natural world.

We deal with socio-economic, political, and environmental patterns at all levels of development. With high quality technology at our disposal, we can develop geospatial concepts that are guaranteed to meet your organizations needs.

Our Technology

At GCC we deal with many technologically demanding projects, so it is crucial to have satellites, LiDAR imaging devices, and software that can operate effectively and accurately.

We use satellites that can acquire up to 50cm high resolution from the Pleiades Satellite.

Our LiDAR imaging technology consists of a fleet of drones, each mounted with a LiDARit M600PRO camera. This allows us to collect remotely sensed data with an accuracy of up to 25mm RMS at 50m.
